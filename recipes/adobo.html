ADOBO

Classic Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 1/2 cup cane vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 3-4 bay leaves
  • 1 stp whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt(only if needed)

How to Make

  1. Marinate the Chicken

    2. In a bowl, mix chicken, soy sauce, garlic, and pepper. Marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour(or overnight for deeper flavor).

  2. Brown the chicken (optional but recommended)

    3. Heat oil in a pan. Remove chicken from marinade and lightly brown both sides. Set aside

  3. Simmer

    4. In the same pan, add the marinade, water, bay leaves, and vinegar. Do NOT stir for the first 3-5 minutes after adding vinegar (important for authentic taste)

  4. Cook

    5. Let it simmer on medium-low heat for 25-30 minutes, until chicken is tender.

    .
  5. Season & reduce sauce

    6. Add sugar if using. Taste and adjust. Simmer uncovered until the sauce thickens

  6. Serve

    7. Best served with steamed rice
