CALDERETA

Classic Beef Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef (brisket or chuck), cut into cubes
  • 2 cups beef broth or water
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup liver spread
  • 1 large potato, cubed
  • 1 large carrot, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2–3 pcs bay leaves
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: green peas, chili, cheese

How to Make

  1. Sauté aromatics

    2. Heat oil in a pot. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.

  2. Brown the beef

    3. Add beef cubes and cook until lightly browned on all sides.

  3. Add liquids & simmer

    4. Pour in tomato sauce and beef broth. Add bay leaves. Simmer for 1 to 1.5 hours or until beef is tender.

  4. Add liver spread

    5. Stir in liver spread to thicken the sauce and deepen the flavor.

  5. Add vegetables

    6. Add potatoes and carrots. Cook for 10 minutes. Add bell peppers and optional ingredients like peas or cheese.

  6. Season

    7. Add salt and pepper to taste. Simmer until sauce thickens and vegetables are cooked.

  7. Serve

    8. Best enjoyed with steamed rice.
