SISIG

Classic Pork Sisig

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly (or pork face if traditional)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup calamansi or lemon juice
  • 2–3 pcs chili (siling labuyo), chopped
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise (optional but recommended)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: egg, liver spread, green onions

How to Make

  1. Boil the pork

    2. Boil pork in salted water until tender. This usually takes 45–60 minutes.

  2. Grill or pan-sear

    3. Grill the pork until slightly charred for authentic flavor. If no grill, pan-sear until browned.

  3. Chop

    4. Chop the pork into small cubes. Traditional sisig is finely chopped.

  4. Sauté

    5. In a pan, melt butter. Sauté garlic and onions until soft. Add chopped pork and mix well.

  5. Season

    6. Add soy sauce, calamansi/lemon juice, and chili. Stir and cook for 3–5 minutes.

  6. Add creaminess

    7. Mix in mayonnaise or liver spread if using. Adjust salt and pepper.

  7. Serve

    8. Serve on a sizzling plate if available. Top with egg and green onions.
